Statistics

Introduction

This chapter gives a brief insight into statistical data of call and confirmation records. For call records, the statistical data is the call ticket switch. For confirmation records, the statistical data are the confirmation ticket switch, the EventCause and EventEffect ticket fields and the transparent data. For a more detailed description please refer to 6.5 / 22.1 Ticketing Specification provided with the product.

Purpose

Statistics provide information on call behavior in the Optiva Railway services and allow the control of administrative tasks.

Record types

Different types of records are generated on the SEP:

  • Call records contain all information related to a call, e.g. CgPA and CdPA. The contents depends on the statistics profile and on the available information.
  • Confirmation records refer to administrative actions. They are written whenever an administrative action is performed and depend on the event(s) which occurred during the call. They also include error information.

For more detailed information on statistics, please refer to the product-specific statistics manuals.
