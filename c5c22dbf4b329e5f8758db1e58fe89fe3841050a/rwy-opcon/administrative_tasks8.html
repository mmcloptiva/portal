Administrative Tasks

Administrative tasks

Administrating the deregistration functionality involves the following administrative tasks:

  • Administration of deregistration data

  • Manual deregistration via the Web-based GUI or Batch is possible. However, it is strongly recommended to use the USSD mechanism.

    For manual deregistration delete the user’s MSISDN from the Msisdn field in the FN record in the Function Register data set.

  • Administration of delayed deregistration
